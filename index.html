Who are we?

For almost all of conscious civilization, humanity has sought to consolidate for survival- perhaps even more necessary for its seemingly self-aware, Smart cohort. Funny how even the most profound of us still innately subscribe to something as mundane and blatantly unevolved as Herdism. So, in the face of this irony, we're here together: to gather, to conceive, and to create. We're unashamedly primal in our pledge to altruism; we're the obnoxious blotch in the face of society's linen: We're MandelBrot.
